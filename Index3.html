The First Year Center offers several orientation programs for new students.

Bear Beginnings: New Student Fall Orientations is required for all fall incoming students. This mandatory four-day program includes Convocation, the First Year Reading Program, and the opportunity to connect with your academic division. Optional programming is also provided for family members. No registration is required for this program.

Winter Welcome: New Student Spring Orientation is a four-day orientation program that assists spring incoming students with their academic, personal, social and cultural transition into the Washington University and St. Louis community.

SOAR : Summer Orientation Advising Registration is an optional three-day program for fall incoming students who would like to explore campus, meet with their advisor in-person, and register for classes. Registration will open at 3:00 PM CST on April 1, 2016.

Pre-Orientation is an optional four-day program for fall incoming students to explore a campus organization or University department. Registration will open at 3:00 PM CST on May 2, 2016.

To learn more about each of these programs, please select the appropriate heading.
